FAQ


Why won't my textbook appear in the searchbar?

To stay up-to-date and help students quickly find the textbooks they need, our site only sells textbooks taught at UVA for the current semester. If your textbook does not appear in the searchbar, it may not be used in this semester's classes.


I just saw a book in the buy page! Where did it go?

When someone adds a book to their cart, it is removed from the available book listings. Someone else might have bought it first. Sorry!
