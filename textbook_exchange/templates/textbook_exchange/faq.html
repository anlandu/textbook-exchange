Frequently Asked Questions

To stay up-to-date and help students quickly find the textbooks they need, our site only lists textbooks taught at UVA for the current semester. If your textbook does not appear in the searchbar, it may not be used in this semester's classes. Additionally, we do not have the ability to sell eBooks and access codes, which some classes may require.
Our site only lists the textbooks and courses provided by the UVA Bookstore Web List, so this book may not be registered in the bookstore and thus on our website.
This class may only need ebooks, access codes or a combination of an access code and a textbook, which our site does not provide. We only allow the selling of physical textbooks. If you need an ebook, try this website: https://uvabookstores.redshelf.com/ .
When someone adds a book to their cart, it is removed from the available book listings. Someone else might have bought it first. Sorry! Don't worry though, once an item is added to someone's cart they have 15 minutes to finish the checkout process or it will be removed from their cart.
Based off comprehensive feedback, we found that most UVA students preferred to meet with the other student face to face when buying and selling the textbook. After (or before) purchasing the textook, just message the seller to decide when and where to meet to exchange the textbook! We suggest meeting during the daytime in public places, such as the Lawn and other places on Grounds.
We don't want anyone to be scammed. As a result, we have an escrow system in place. After placing an order, the buyer has two weeks to raise concerns such as "I never got my textbook!" and "This textbook is not in as good of condition as I was told!". During this time the money has not been given to the seller yet, and if you return the textbook to the seller or demonstrate to us that you never received it, you will get all your money back.

If you have a problem with your order for any reason, don't hesitate to use the Contact Us form! We will be in contact within 48 hours about next steps and what to expect!
